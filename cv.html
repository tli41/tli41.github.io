Ting Li, Ph.D.

I am an Assistant Professor in Computer Science at Oxford College of Emory University. My research focuses on mobile crowd sensing, edge computing, privacy-preserving systems, indoor localization, and bike-sharing analytics. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from UNC Charlotte (2019), advised by Dr. Yu Wang.
